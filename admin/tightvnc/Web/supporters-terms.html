Get Listed at TightVNC
 
Improve TightVNC:
Make a Donation!

Everyone who made a donation within past three months can be listed in the TightVNC Hall of Fame. However, making a donation does not automatically list you on that page. To add your name or your Web site to the list, please perform the following steps:

  • Read and understand the list of Terms and Conditions included below.
  • Make a donation, if you did not do that yet.
  • Fill in and e-mail us an application form, as described at the bottom of this page.

Terms and Conditions

  1. Only the donations made during the most recent 3 months are considered when publishing information about the supporter. The payments should represent voluntary donations to the TightVNC project. Payments or compensations for any products, licenses or services are not taken into account. The record is likely to be removed if 3 months have passed since the most recent donation.
  2. The list of supporters is sorted by the sum donated in past three months (bigger sums first). Records with equal sums are then sorted by the date of the most recent donation (most recent donations first).
  3. Publishing a clickable link to a Web site requires that the sum is at least USD 100. For simple name and/or company listing, USD 10 donation is sufficient.
  4. Submissions are processed manually so there may be a delay before the information can be published.
  5. TightVNC Web site owners reserve the right to refuse to publish any information or links to any information they consider inappropriate. In particular, we certainly will not publish links to online gambling sites, sex sites, scam and pyramid schemes, religious propaganda etc. In the case of such refusal, we reserve the right not to describe the reasons for the decision. If you are not sure if your link is acceptable, inquire via e-mail.
  6. Donation to the TightVNC project is not a payment for publishing a link or any other information on the TightVNC site. Publishing such information is a right, not an obligation of the TightVNC Web site owners.
  7. No information is published without explicit intent of the supporter who fills in and sends the corresponding application form. E-mail addresses are not published or disclosed, unless forced by local or international laws.

Applications

Applications should be submitted to the e-mail address sapp@tightvnc.com, in text-only messages encoded in Latin or UTF-8. Here is an example of such a message:

To: sapp@tightvnc.com
Subject: Application
I would like to include the following information to the list of TightVNC supporters:

Name: John Doe
Site/Company: John's Company
Link: http://www.johns-company.com/

Donations History (for past 3 months):
1. $100 donation on 2008-07-16 TransactionID CH4R2UJDV9HD
2. $10 donation on 2008-06-01 TransactionID CH4F1RVXH22N

I have read, understood and agreed with the Terms and Conditions document published at
http://www.tightvnc.com/supporters-terms.html

Just copy the example above in the newly created e-mail message, replace sample data with your actual information, and send the e-mail. If everything is good, you will receive a reply in the nearest days.

Thank you for supporting the TightVNC Project!
